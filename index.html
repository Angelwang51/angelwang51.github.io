ABOUT

I’ve got three majors and worked through different industries and jobs until I finally realized that I’m way too interested in coding and designing.

I started my professional career as a Business Analyst at a bank 4 years ago. I was assigned occasionally to a project with IT department. It soon became apparent that I really enjoyed spending time with the developers and understanding how they do their magic. I started trying it myself and I naturally learned some skills of front end development and design. I started to enjoy people call me “nerd”.

After I went back to school to study IT, I embarked on a freelance career which gave me the freedom to work on all sorts of projects with different kinds of business – from online jewelry store, to reading application and blog.

I strive in busy environments and on interesting projects. I want to be involved in projects that I can be proud of, join an amazing team, and advance my design and development career. Take some time to look at my work, credentials, and if you're interested in helping me make this happen, let's get in touch.
